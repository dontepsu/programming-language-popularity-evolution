This animated bubble chart visualizes the evolution of programming language popularity using Stack Overflow Developer Survey data.

The x-axis shows the relative usage frequency, and the y-axis shows interest (developers who want to use a language). Both axes use a logarithmic scale to reduce clutter and improve visibility of smaller values.

Each bubble's size represents the average reported salary for that language. The color encodes a cluster based on the language's memory management strategy and whether it's compiled or interpreted.

You can click a language bubble to track or untrack it during the animation. This lets you follow a specific language's trajectory across years.
